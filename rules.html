Rules

Discord:

  • Be respectful to other members. This includes refraining from personal attacks, hate speech, and discrimination of any kind.
  • Keep discussions and content appropriate for all ages. No NSFW (not safe for work) content is allowed.
  • Do not spam or flood the chat. This includes excessive use of emotes, all caps messages, and repetitive messages.
  • Do not advertise or promote other Discord servers, products, or services without prior permission from the server owner or moderators.
  • Do not share personal information of others without their explicit consent.
  • Respect the authority of the moderators and administrators. If a moderator asks you to stop doing something, comply with their request.
  • Do not impersonate other members, staff, or bots.

Minecraft:

  • All Chat Rules from the Discord section.
  • No cheating, hacking, exploiting or glitching is allowed.
